What is 2S Racing league?

We have some of the most epic on-track battles, strong fields, and great rivalries! We cater for players across a wide range of skill, and if you're looking for more than just racing, such as a full on gaming community, then the 2S Community is the place for you! Check out our discord to sign up and view information about the current Season, or just talk within the community. You can also have a look at some of our live-streamed, commentated races on our youtube channel, also make sure to check out our instagram for weekly updates of our standings and pictures from the races!
